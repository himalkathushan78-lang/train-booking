Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a booking?

Contact support with your ticket reference number before departure and we'll sort it out.

How do I get my e-ticket?

Your e-ticket downloads right after booking is confirmed. A copy also goes to your registered mobile number.

Can I change my travel date?

Yes, you can change dates up to 24 hours before travel, as long as seats are still available on that day.
